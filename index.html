About

Where Technology Meets Business Strategy

I'm Felipe Duarte, a Data & AI Executive at Itau Unibanco — the largest financial institution in Latin America. I lead the data strategy across Cards, Current Accounts, and Payment Methods for the entire Retail Banking division.

My career uniquely bridges deep technical expertise and executive leadership. With a PhD in Machine Learning from the University of Sao Paulo and hands-on experience across Data Engineering, Data Science, ML Engineering, Analytics Engineering, and Data Governance, I bring a rare combination of scientific rigor and business acumen.

Over the past decade, I've built and led data organizations at Brazil's most prestigious banks — Itau Unibanco, Santander, and BTG Pactual — transforming how these institutions leverage data and AI to create competitive advantage.
